How to find your UUID

The UUID is unique to each Animal Jam account. You must capture your network traffic while logging into Animal Jam.
Download and setup Proxyman, and start capturing. Then, on the same computer, log into Animal Jam. Stop the capture.
You should find a request/response similar to the image below, showing your account's UUID.
